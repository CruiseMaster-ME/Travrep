OUR SERVICES

Sales

Our crucial service involves focusing on sales and revenue through face-to-face calls and authorised representation with decision makers. We also assist with negotiation of contracts, CRM, enhancing brand awareness and product visibility.

Marketing

To ensure optimum brand awareness and product visibility, we will manage the multiple facets of marketing through tactics such as inclusion in sales collateral, periodic newsletters and e-blasts, social media coverage via our own platforms, dedicated page on the TravReps website and many more

Business Development

Conducted in tandem with our sales and marketing efforts, we analyse and improve operational procedures and practises to suit the Middle Eastern market. Given our experience, we can provide market intelligence and competitor analysis for optimum business expansion and new product development

Public Relations

An extension to marketing efforts, the focus on PR is to intensify the brand awareness and share of voice through close connections with the press. We can customise the PR campaign to suit KPIs, and generate exposure through press content development and dissemination, building robust relationships with the media and conducting workshops and trade shows to elevate brand presence

Consulting

Several years of experience have equipped us with a deep understanding of the industry, region and its markets. If you are interested in testing the waters prior to making a full-fledged business commitment, you may benefit from our expertise on an ad-hoc basis via our market and competitor analysis, business development skills, sales and marketing plans and much more
